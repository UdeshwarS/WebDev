Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do you provide a car for the road test?

Yes, a car is available for both G2 and G road tests as part of the package or upon request for an additional charge.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off?

Yes, free pick-up and drop-off is included for all lessons within the service area.

How many lessons do I need?

It depends on your experience level. Beginners may need more lessons, while experienced drivers may only need a few for test preparation.

Do you offer packages?

Yes, discounted packages are available which include multiple lessons and road test preparation.
