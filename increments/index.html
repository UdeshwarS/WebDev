🎮 HOW TO PLAY 🎮


🪙 Collect Coins

  • Click the Question Box to collect coins
  • Each Click Gives you coins based on your current Coins Per Click value

⬆ Buy Upgrades

  • Use coins to purchase upgrades from the Upgrade Store
  • The Price increases each time you buy one
  • Upgrades can either increase the number of coins you get per click (+1, +3, or + 10 clicks) or unlock automatic coin generation, which will click for you every x seconds, where x represents the time interval between automatic clicks and decreases with higher upgrade levels.

🏆 Earn Rewards

  • Earn Rewards by reaching milestones:
  • Collect 100 Coins
  • Buy your first upgrade
  • Unlock auto-click
  • Buy 25 upgrades
  • Collect 100,000 Coins
